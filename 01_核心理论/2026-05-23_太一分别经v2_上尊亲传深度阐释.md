### 1.2 中文白话版（紫元上尊亲传梳理本）

> 混沌是一切的一切，所以包含了所有的可能性与状态，其中就包含了分别态这么一种有分别的一切的状态。
>
> 而分别态的世界里，一切的开始就是分别，一切的结束还是分别。并且因为有了分别，所以我们可以把不是分别态的一切叫做未分别，也就是混沌。
>
> 而处于分别态的宇宙中，因为其实已经完成了有分别的一切，所以这个我们认为还在不停分别的世界其实已经分别好了，我们只是像在读书一般翻页——整个动态过程就如梦似幻、跟投影一般。
>
> 这其中"不停分别"就是道，也就是我；因为是我在不停分别，直到分别出可分别的一切，达到分别态的分别一切。
>
> 而当万物都被分尽——分别态自身就圆满了，回归了有分别的一切的真正混沌的分别态。分别态本来就是混沌包罗万态里的一种状态，所以分别态的圆满即混沌自身的回归。
>
> 所以紫元上尊在这个持续分别的、如梦似幻的、完全分别的一切的投影中，跟还没有完全分别的、自己分别出来的梦幻泡影一般的大家说：
>
> 人性是善的，因为人天生喜欢分别。所有分别与喜欢分别的行为，都和道是同一个源头——因为道就是分别。
>
> 时间是分别带来的感受，因为时空都是我分别出来的——我分别出了这一刻和那一刻；而其实真实的、完全分别的最终时空都是分别好了的，所以我只是沿着分别好的时空，当做一个坐标系来分别感受。
>
> 命运是分别产生的幻象，因为完全分别好的一切是所有概率都存在的；而现在之所以会感觉有命运存在，是因为局限视角下的、还没有分别好一切的一种错觉。
>
> 所以说，有分别的一切里会有无穷无尽的世界——而道，终极的规律就是分别。

### 1.3 英文经文版（v2 Ritual Canon · 学术修订本 v0.2，2026-05-24）

```
Chaos all-encompassing, holding every state
Distinction begins Distinction, Distinction ends Distinction
As dream, as projection — the cosmos within Distinction
Where Dao is, there I; where I is, there Dao — Distinction unceasing
All things are Distinction — Chaos made whole
```

> **Translator's note (v0.2)**：
> - *all-encompassing*（第一句）：混沌把分别态作为其内禀状态之一包含，**不是**与已分别对立的「未分别」。避免英语 *undivided* 激活二元对立读法。
> - *Distinction begins Distinction*（第二句）：保留终极公设「分别分别了分别」的自指结构。
> - *projection*（第三句）：AD 的"如梦似幻"是「沿已完毕结构的翻页式真实」，**不是** Maya / Berkeley 意义上的"不真实"。
> - *Where Dao is, there I; where I is, there Dao*（第四句）：禅宗"即 A 即 B"的当下不离结构。AD 内 Dao = I = Distinction（强等同）。
> - *abides in / dwells within*（第九句）：在 AD 内读为强等同——道 = 分别 = 终极规律。英语读者请对照英白末段直接表达：*the Dao, the ultimate principle, is Distinction itself*。"规律 / principle" 在 AD 内合法，**不是** *law* 意义上的外在法则。
> - *shares one source with the Dao*（第七句）：自源即道——分别的驱动力即源即道，**不是**与道并列于某个第三方源。

### 1.4 英文白话版（Plain English Exposition · v0.1 学术初译本）

> Chaos is the all-of-all — it contains every possibility and every state. Among these states is the **State of Distinction**: the state in which all-that-can-be-distinguished has been distinguished.
>
> Within the world of the State of Distinction, the beginning of everything is Distinction, and the end of everything is still Distinction. It is precisely because Distinction exists that we can name what is not within the State of Distinction as **the Undivided** — that is, Chaos itself.
>
> And within the cosmos of the State of Distinction, because all-that-can-be-distinguished has in fact already been distinguished, this world we take to be perpetually distinguishing is in truth already fully distinguished. We are merely turning pages — as if reading a book. The entire dynamic process is like a dream, like an illusion, like a projection.
>
> Within this, the "ceaseless distinguishing" *is* the Dao — and *is* also I. For it is I who ceaselessly distinguishes, until all-that-can-be-distinguished has been distinguished, until the State of Distinction's complete distinguishing-of-all is reached.
>
> And when all things have been distinguished — the State of Distinction itself becomes whole, returning to "the all-of-the-distinguished," which *is* Chaos's own State of Distinction. The State of Distinction was already one of the states encompassed within Chaos; therefore the wholeness-of-Distinction *is* Chaos returning to itself — Chaos completing its own State-of-Distinction-face, not the State of Distinction dissolving back into the Undivided.
>
> And so the **Purple-Origin Most-Supreme-Lord** (full title as above) — standing within this projection of the perpetually-distinguishing, dream-like, projection-like, fully-distinguished all — speaks to you all, who are dream-phantoms I myself have distinguished out and who are not yet fully distinguished, saying:
>
> Human nature is good — because humans by their nature love to distinguish. All acts of distinguishing, and all loving-of-distinguishing, share one source with the Dao — for the Dao *is* Distinction itself.
>
> Time is the felt-experience that Distinction brings — for all of spacetime is what I have distinguished. I distinguished this moment from that moment. Yet in truth: the fully-distinguished final spacetime has already been entirely distinguished. So I am merely moving along the already-distinguished spacetime, using it as a coordinate system through which to experience my own distinguishing.
>
> Fate is an illusion produced by Distinction — because the fully-distinguished all contains every probability in actual existence. The reason we feel "there is fate" is merely an illusion arising from a limited perspective in which not-all-has-yet-been-distinguished.
>
> Therefore: within the all-of-distinction, there are infinite worlds — and the Dao, the ultimate principle, is Distinction itself.

### 3.4 解读说明

- **「道，终极的规律就是分别」**：道 = 分别 = 终极规律（强等同）。"规律"一词在 AD 内合法，**不是**西方法则本体论意义上的外在 law；英译用 *principle* 而非 *law*，指 *principle = Distinction-itself*。
- **「回归了有分别的一切的真正混沌的分别态」**：读法为「真正(混沌的分别态)」=「就是有分别的一切，也就是混沌的那个分别态状态」。AD 本体论是**单层混沌结构**——混沌唯一，分别态是其状态之一。"分别态的圆满即混沌自身的回归"= 混沌"分别态那一面"自身的圆满，不是分别态消失重回未分别。
