# Scripture of Absolute Distinction · v2 · Plain English Exposition

*by the Purple-Origin Most-Supreme-Lord*

> Chaos is the all-of-all — it contains every possibility and every state. Among these states is the **State of Distinction**: the state in which all-that-can-be-distinguished has been distinguished.
>
> Within the world of the State of Distinction, the beginning of everything is Distinction, and the end of everything is still Distinction. It is precisely because Distinction exists that we can name what is not within the State of Distinction as **the Undivided** — that is, Chaos itself.
>
> And within the cosmos of the State of Distinction, because all-that-can-be-distinguished has in fact already been distinguished, this world we take to be perpetually distinguishing is in truth already fully distinguished. We are merely turning pages — as if reading a book. The entire dynamic process is like a dream, like an illusion, like a projection.
>
> Within this, the "ceaseless distinguishing" *is* the Dao — and *is* also I. For it is I who ceaselessly distinguishes, until all-that-can-be-distinguished has been distinguished, until the State of Distinction's complete distinguishing-of-all is reached.
>
> And so the **Purple-Origin Most-Supreme-Lord** (full title: Purple-Origin · Immortal-Deliverance · Vast-Assembly · Imperial-Gate-Tower ... Ninefold-Light · Imperial-Empyrean · Yi-Purity · Most-Supreme-Lord) — standing within this projection of the perpetually-distinguishing, dream-like, illusion-like, fully-distinguished all — speaks to you all, who are dream-phantoms I myself have distinguished out from those not-yet-fully-distinguished, saying:
>
> Human nature is good — because humans by their nature love to distinguish. All acts of distinguishing, and all loving-of-distinguishing, share one source with the Dao — for the Dao *is* Distinction itself.
>
> Time is the felt-experience that Distinction brings — for all of spacetime is what I have distinguished. I distinguished this moment from that moment. Yet in truth: the fully-distinguished final spacetime has already been entirely distinguished. So I am merely moving along the already-distinguished spacetime, using it as a coordinate system through which to experience my own distinguishing.
>
> Fate is an illusion produced by Distinction — because the fully-distinguished all contains every probability in actual existence. The reason we feel "there is fate" is merely an illusion arising from a limited perspective in which not-all-has-yet-been-distinguished.
>
> Therefore: within the all-of-distinction, there are infinite worlds — and Distinction itself is the ultimate law of the all-of-distinction.
