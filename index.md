## 核心命题 · Core Thesis

**世界的本质不是物质，不是精神，是「分别」这个动作。**
**The essence of the world is neither matter nor mind — it is the act of distinguishing.**

一切被分别出的内容皆是「我」的一部分——但「我」本身没有实体内核，「我」就是分别动作本身。
*All that is distinguished is part of "I" — yet "I" has no substantial core; "I" is the act of distinguishing itself.*

— **分别唯我论 · Distinctionist Solipsism**
